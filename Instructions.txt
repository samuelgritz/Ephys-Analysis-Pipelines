How to use this workflow
1. Run Analyze_and_Export_... for specific experiment. This creates the clean CSVs in paper_data.

2. Run Analyze_Stats_Python.py.

It will automatically load those CSVs.

It will perform the Shapiro-Wilk test on every comparison.

It will automatically switch between T-Test and Mann-Whitney.

Or do any other stats you want.

It will print a clean report to your terminal with p-values and significance stars.

3. Update generate_figures.py:

Take the p-values printed by the stats script.

Manually add them to your add_stat_annotation(...) calls in the plotting script.
